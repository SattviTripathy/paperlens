FAQ & Install

Install on Android

  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right).
  3. Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  4. Confirm — PaperLens lands on your home screen and opens full-screen like a native app.

Install on iPhone & iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari — iOS only allows installing from Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an up arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — the app icon appears on your home screen.

How good is text extraction (OCR)?

OCR is experimental for now. It works best on clean, high-contrast printed text. Handwriting, small fonts, and poorly lit photos come out unreliable. Treat extracted text as a rough draft — accuracy will improve in future updates.

Is my data private?

Yes. Edge detection, cleanup, OCR, and PDF building all run on your device. No photos or text ever leave your browser — there's no server and no account.

Does it work offline?

After the first load, yes. The app and its libraries are cached, so you can scan without a connection. The camera and installation need the page to be opened over https.
